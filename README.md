# 📊 RetailMart Sales Tableau Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
The RetailMart Sales Tableau Dashboard is an interactive business intelligence project that analyzes retail sales data to uncover valuable insights into sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, product performance, and regional trends. The dashboard enables users to explore key business metrics through dynamic visualizations and interactive filters.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze overall sales performance.
- Monitor profit and revenue trends.
- Identify top-performing products and categories.
- Compare regional sales performance.
- Understand customer purchasing behavior.
- Support business decisions through interactive dashboards.

---
